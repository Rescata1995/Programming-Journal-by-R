sort1 uses: Bubble sort

How do you know?: It is the one that takes the longest in random listings; but, the one that takes less time in already sorted listings.

sort2 uses: Merge sort

How do you know?: It takes the least time for random listings, especially very large listings.

sort3 uses: Selection sort

How do you know?: It is the one that takes the longest in already ordered listings, especially in very large listings.
